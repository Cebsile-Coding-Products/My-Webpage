School Performance

I come from the Kingdom of Eswatini. I school at Sifundzani Primary School in the capital Mbabane. I live in Mbabane. At school we learn a lot of subjects. I am excellent in all of them and I have topped my class for the past 3 years. I am also good in sports. I have won 6 team trophies and 16 medals respectively.

  • Cricket: 1 Gold, 2 Silver, 0 Bronze and 1 Participation - 4 Medals
  • Soccer: 2 Gold, 0 Silver, 0 Bronze - 2 Medals

  • Tennis: 1 Gold, 1 Silver, 1 Bronze - 3 Medals
  • Athletics: 0 Gold, 1 Silver, 0 Bronze - 1 Medal

I have also won an individial trophy in Hockey for Top Goal Scorer. I love sports. They are lots of fun. There is a lot of competetion so other people are able to challenge and it sometimes pushes me to my limit which I like. But there is no feeling that beats winning a competetion and taking the trophy back to school. Sports also help to make friends from other schools.
